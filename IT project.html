IT project:ONLINE EDUCATION WITHOUT THE INTERNET

Project Introduction:Many people in the world do not have access to the Internet. This restriction increases the knowledge gap between internet users and those without internet access. We want to store offline educational resources via portable servers and serve content via local (offline) wireless connections. The server comes preloaded with content from educational websites. This content will be repackaged and downloaded for offline use
